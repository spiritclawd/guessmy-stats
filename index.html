When Collector Mode launches, the loser of each game transfers their NFT to the winner. The winner pays a 10% claim fee. 90% of that fee goes directly to buying NFTs from the collection — returning them to the community. The remaining 10% goes to the protocol.
Treasury Strategy

  • 90% of fee → collection NFT buybacks
  • 10% of fee → protocol treasury
Treasury Goals
  • Build protocol-owned NFT floor
  • Fund future collection whitelistings
  • Community buybacks
